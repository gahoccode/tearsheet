How to Use
Stock Symbols

Enter Vietnam stock symbols (e.g., REE, FMC, DHC). These should be valid symbols traded on Ho Chi Minh Stock Exchange (HOSE) or Hanoi Stock Exchange (HNX).

Portfolio Weights

Assign weights to each stock representing their proportion in your portfolio. Weights must be between 0 and 1, and must sum to exactly 1.0 (representing 100% of your portfolio).

Date Range

Select the period for historical analysis. The system will fetch historical price data for this period to calculate portfolio performance.

Initial Capital

Enter your initial investment amount in Vietnamese Dong (VND). This will be used to calculate absolute returns and position sizes.
